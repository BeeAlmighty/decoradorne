# Decor Adorne — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Essence
Decor Adorne transforms spaces into memories. Every event — from intimate Kamu
celebrations to grand engagement parties — is treated as a canvas for beauty.

## Voice & Tone
- Warm, not cold. Aspirational, not inaccessible.
- Celebrate Nigerian cultural events with specificity and pride.
- Never use generic event language. Name the celebration: "your Kamu", "your Nikkah".
- Short sentences. Evocative imagery. No corporate filler.

## Logo Usage
- Minimum size: 120px wide on digital
- Clear space: equal to the height of the "D" in Decor on all sides
- Do not recolor. Do not add drop shadows.
- Dark background variant available for inverted contexts.

## CTA Patterns
- Primary: "Plan my event →"
- Secondary: "Chat on WhatsApp →"
- Lead form submit: "Request a quote →"
- Never: "Click here", "Submit", "Learn more" (generic)

## Photography Style
- Natural light preferred
- Warm tones, no cold filters
- Show detail: flowers, linens, candles — not just wide shots
- Lagos venues preferred for location credibility
